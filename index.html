Heroes examples

Goli soda is a carbonated soft drink from India that's sold in a distinctive Codd-neck bottle with a marble stopper. It's also known as banta soda in North India.

Why it's popular:

Goli soda has been a popular drink in India since the late 19th century It's a nostalgic drink that's making a comeback in India

How it's made

Made with carbonated water, flavored syrup, and sometimes salt The carbonation pressure forces a glass marble into the neck of the bottle, sealing it .
